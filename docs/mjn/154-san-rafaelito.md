# Dive 154: Lobera San Rafaelito, La Paz, BCS, MEX
#### Date: 14 February 2026
#### Latitude: 24°17'52.3"N    
#### Longitude: 110°20'44.9"W
#### Dive Number: 154
#### Overview
##### Type: Ocean, Boat Dive (Panga)
##### Partner: Amy R.


Dove Lobera San Rafaelito, a small rocky outcrop outside of La Paz. Increasingly this is a known colony and rookery for sea lions. We ended up at this location becasue of wind and sea conditions. I get the sense this was not a normal location in the dive rotation. Shallow entry in 3 meters.
Decent amount of live coral. Location exceeds expectations with coral and fish diversity. Fun sea lion interaction near the end of the dive.
