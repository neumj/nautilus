# Dive 146: Emerald Cove (West), Catalina, CA, USA
#### Date: 30 August 2025
#### Latitude: 33°28'07.8"N     
#### Longitude: 118°31'36.9"W 
#### Dive Number: 146
#### Overview
##### Type: Ocean, Boat Dive (Makai Mist)
